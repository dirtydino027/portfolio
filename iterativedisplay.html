iterative design

context:
- for this project i worked with a group of cs students to design an app interface for a startup called Hedgehog, which focuses on using AI robots for mushroom farming
- we began by each sketching designs for three possible pages of the app to consider as many different designs as we could
- we then settled on the designs we wanted, made a style guide, and created high-fi prototypes of eight app pages using Figma
- once we had these initial prototypes, we participated in a studio where our designs were critiqued by other cs students, and then inforporated this feedback into an updated set of 18 app page mockups
- lastly, we conducted user testing to see where and how users' eyes scanned the pages

lessons learned:
- working in a group allows for more diverse design ideas and solutions to issues
- when designing for an organization that you aren't familiar with, it is important to learn about their goals, priorities, vocabulary, etc.
- it can be difficult to make decisions and incorporate everyone's design ideas when working in a group
