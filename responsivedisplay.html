responsive redesign

context:
- for this project i reimagined the interface of a website that i noticed was unintuitive and unresponsive
- after assessing the usability issues of the website, i made low-fi wireframes of my new design ideas for three different formats: desktop, tablet, and mobile. these designs addressed the usability issues present in the original website
- i then created a style guide including the colors, buttons, and text for the final design, and used it to create high-fi mockups for the final website in the same three formats
- finally, i coded the actual website, focusing on responsive and intuitive design

lessons learned:
- incorporating responsiveness into website design is important to account for different size windows and devices
- unresponsive interfaces can be unintuitive and difficult to use
- prioritize simple, intuitive interaces just including the necessary information and links
